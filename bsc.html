Carrier Opportunity :

  • First and easy Opportunity to get placement in service based companies( like tcs,infosys etc) after the graduation.
  • You can take job after graduation in good companies using the Linkedin Referral.
  • You can opt remote work.. trending in these days but you have to do open source contribution and increase your personal branding.
  • You can go for higher studies and the best degree after graduation in IT stream is MCA ...if you want to do MCA then you must prepare for NIMCET.
  • You can do freelancing for clients after learning a skill and master it.
  • You can go with government jobs because the only criteria for every government job is graduation.

Mistakes To Avoid :

  • Learn only one programming language at a time never study multiple languages together it will only confuse you.
  • Don't skip DSA, you can learn the basics no need to deep dive in dsa but solve enough questions to grip on it.
  • Never learn things randomly if you do this,at the end you will learn nothing so make a proper schedule or to do list and complete everything one by one.
  • Don't understimate the aptitude, Give some time for aptitude practice because aptitude is the first part of every interview round.
  • Make sure you also connect with alumni and some good profiles using linkedin and twitter, so that you can ask for help.
  • Don't only focus on your college syllabus or curriculum try to participate in many online events and contests...
